Beef Handi

Description:

Beef Handi Pakistani Food Recipe, it is one of famous in Pakistan and India. It is a portion of very healthy food for your body. Red meat is one of the most nutrient food, you can eat. It is full of vitamins, minerals, antioxidants, and various other nutrients that can profound health. Beef is the source of high-quantity vitamins and nutrients. Everybody has been eating beef in prehistory times.

Ingredients:

  • Olive oil
  • Chopped Onion
  • Bay Leaves
  • Black Pepper
  • Small Cardamom
  • Cinnamon Stick
  • Cumin Seed
  • Beef Meat
  • Salt
  • Red Chili Powder
  • Turmeric Powder
  • Garam Masala Powder
  • Yogurt
  • Green Chilies
  • Black Pepper Powder

Steps:

  1. Take large handi and add three to four tbsp of olive oil and heat on high flame of the stove
  2. Add three medium sizes of chopped onion and cook it
  3. Add bay leaves, black peppers, small cardamom, cinnamon, and cumin seeds
  4. Cook it until then onion can changes the color
  5. Add one kg of beef and cook it
  6. Mix Well
  7. Add spices like Salt, two tbsp of coriander powder, two tbsp of red chili powder, and half tbsp of turmeric powder
  8. Add spices into the beef handi
  9. Add four cups of water and boiled it
  10. Cook for 30 to 35 minutes
  11. After 30 minutes, water gets dry
  12. Add half cup of yogurt and mix well
  13. Add 5 to 7 green chilies
  14. In the end, add small cardamom powder, and black pepper powder and cook into low flame of the stove for 5 minutes
  15. Cook it and water gets dry
  16. Delicious and tasty beef handi is ready to eat
  17. Serves with Naan, chapatti, and rice
